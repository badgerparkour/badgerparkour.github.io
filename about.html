About Us

Badger Parkour, founded in May 2023 by Jake and Connor, is the coolest club at UW-Madison. We are beginner friendly, and we do so much more than just parkour. We do tricking, climbing, gymnastics, juggling, ice skating, and circus. We like to explore campus, ride bikes, and have socials with other clubs. We encourage everyone to enjoy more and worry less.


Practices: Daily @ Gymnastics, otherwise TBD.
Exploring: Weekly, whenever you want!
Socials: A few times per semester.
